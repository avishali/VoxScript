MISSION ID: OPTIMIZE_PLAYBACK_ALLOC_V1

================================================================================
BACKGROUND
================================================================================
The previous mission (AUDIO_PASSTHROUGH_FIX) successfully implemented audio
passthrough logic but failed the Real-Time Safety audit because it allocated
a `juce::AudioBuffer` inside `processBlock()`.

We must move this allocation to `prepareToPlay()` to ensure the audio thread
is allocation-free.

================================================================================
OBJECTIVE
================================================================================
Modify VoxScriptPlaybackRenderer to use a pre-allocated member buffer instead
of creating a temporary buffer on the stack (which unknowingly allocates heap
memory) every callback.
